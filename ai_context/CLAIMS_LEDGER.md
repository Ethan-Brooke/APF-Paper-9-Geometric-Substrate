# CLAIMS_LEDGER — Paper 9: The Geometric Substrate as Cost Structure of Comparison Continuations

## Status tag legend

- **[P]** — proved within Paper 9 or its supplement
- **[P+lattice]** — proved using lattice QCD as an external input
- **[P+IJC]** — proved conditional on the IJC branch (companion supplement)
- **[C]** — conjecture; load-bearing but not formally proved
- **[E]** — empirical fit; not a derivation
- **[N]** — not claimed (out of scope)
